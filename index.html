Our Goals:

DELIVER A PAYLOAD

Design a rocket that cradles two sideways oriented eggs

REACH A SET ALTITUDE

Aim for a maximum altitude of exactly 790 feet

FLY FOR A SET TIME

Remain in flight within the range of 41 to 44 seconds

RETURN TO GROUND

Safely return to the ground with no damage to the eggs
